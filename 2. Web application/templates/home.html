Analysis of student confession page NUSWhispers

NUSWhispers is the popular student confession page of the National University of Singapore.
Given the variety of confessions, I thought it would be interesting to find out answers to questions such as
"what are the most popular topics people talk about" or "how do people feel about finding jobs".
This analysis covers 44,443 confessions made from Apr 2015 to Oct 2019. Hope you enjoy! :)
