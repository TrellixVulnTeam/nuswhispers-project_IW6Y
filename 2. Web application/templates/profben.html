Prof Ben popularity

Professor Ben Leong is a popular Computer Science professor in NUS, known for his dedication to help students and his wise advice.
Out of late, there have been many confessions asking Prof Ben for advice. Thus, I was curious to
find out when Prof Ben became so popular and what topics people usually consult him for.

Popularity of Prof Ben across the years

One way we can find out about his popularity is to identify the number of times he was mentioned in confessions.

From the above graph, we can see a general increase in his popularity from 2015 to 2019, with a surge from 2018 to 2019.
This made me wonder: what are people asking Prof Ben about in 2019?

Popular topics people ask Prof Ben about in 2019

To identify the topics, we can group together words to uncover common themes.

It appears that most people consult Prof Ben regarding career/work.
Interestingly, around 43% of all confessions involve Computer Science. This makes sense since Prof Ben is a CS professor.
However, this is also correlated with the increase in CS confessions, which most likely explains the surge in Prof Ben confessions in 2019.
