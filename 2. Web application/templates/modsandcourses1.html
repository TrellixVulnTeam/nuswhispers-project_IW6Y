Modules and Courses

On NUSWhispers, people often ask about and share about modules.
We want to find out which are the most popular modules and why they are being mentioned.
Furthermore, since the modules have different levels (basic/advanced), we can identify
the type of students belonging to one course and plot their journey on NUSWhispers.

Distribution of top modules mentioned

We can observe that Computer Science takes up a huge proportion of mentions,
followed by Math and a mix of sciences and general education modules.
We can look at the top ranked modules of each year to have a better idea of which modules are popular.

Ranking of top modules mentioned

Before we proceed with the analysis, I want to briefly introduce you to the method I used to calculate mentions.
This is because it will affect the accuracy of the analysis, as well as provide a different insight.

Method 2 is more accurate to calculate top ranked modules.
Imagine that one confession is ranting about a particular module and mentions it 5 times.
Using method 1, this will greatly inflate the ranking of a module, compared to method 2.

Nevertheless, method 1 still has its use, since we can compare the rankings using method 1 vs method 2
to identify the modules that have multiple mentions in one confession.
This can indicate that the confessions relating to the module may be of a different nature (eg. a rant),
such that that the writer mentions the module multiple times.
