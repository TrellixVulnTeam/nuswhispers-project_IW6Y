We can achieve this by identifying common keywords across confessions, and then inferring the topic.

Current Issues confessions across the years

As current issues are different each year, I thought it would be interesting to look at the top current issues confessions each year.

It seems that confessions about current issues appear to have less engagement compared to other topics.
This can be worrying since current issues are important to the development of our society.

Looks like orientation scandals started as early as 2016, and the issue resurfaced in 2019.
Hopefully, we have developed as a society in our maturity to discuss and resolve this issue.
The saga about a young couple arguing with an old man over seats in 2017 reminds me of the recent incident in 2019
where a condominium owner shouted vulgarities at a security officer over parking fees.
As a society, we have strong reactions towards such issues: hate comments towards the couple
and a petition to get the condo owner fired from his company.

LGBT rights are one of the most discussed current issues in 2015. This is likely due to the Singapore Supreme Court
upholding Section 377A (ban on same-sex relations between consenting adult men) in Oct 2014.

Over the years, different issues have emerged, challenging our society.
Hopefully, we will continue to mature as a society to discuss and resolve these issues.
